Sustainable Development for a Better Planet

At Daksh Foundation, we believe that true development must be sustainable, inclusive, and planet-friendly. As part of our environmental initiatives, we are driving impactful projects that not only protect ecosystems but also generate verifiable Carbon Credits, enabling companies to meet their CSR and ESG goals effectively.

Our Carbon Credit Initiative

Climate change is one of the greatest challenges of our time. Through our Carbon Credit programs, Daksh Foundation partners with communities, industries, and corporate CSR teams to reduce carbon footprints and offset emissions through measurable, science-backed projects.

Afforestation and Reforestation Projects

Plantation drives across rural and semi-urban India for carbon sequestration and biodiversity enhancement.

Renewable Energy Promotion

Supporting solar and biogas adoption in communities to replace carbon-intensive fuels.

Waste Management and Circular Economy

Initiatives for waste segregation, recycling, and composting to minimize methane emissions.

Water Conservation and Wetland Restoration

Reviving traditional water bodies and wetlands to restore ecological balance.

Each project is designed to generate carbon offsets while delivering tangible social benefits such as livelihood generation, clean air, and sustainable agriculture.

CSR Partnership Opportunities

Daksh Foundation collaborates with corporates, institutions, and development agencies to integrate Carbon Credit projects into their CSR portfolios. Through our expert-led execution and transparent reporting, partners can:

Fulfill CSR Mandates

Fulfill CSR mandates under environmental sustainability (Schedule VII, Companies Act 2013).

Achieve ESG Goals

Achieve ESG and Net Zero goals.

Gain Visibility

Gain visibility through impact stories, dashboards, and carbon footprint reports.

Contribute to NDCs

Contribute to India’s Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs) under the Paris Agreement.
